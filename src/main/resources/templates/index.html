CivicConnect

Empowering citizens to report issues and engage with local government for better community services.

A secure portal for citizens to report public service issues, track resolution progress, and rate services.

Login to Dashboard
Report Issues

Easily report problems with public services like potholes, streetlights, garbage collection, and more.

Track Progress

Monitor the status of your reported issues and receive updates from municipal staff.

Rate Services

Provide feedback on how well issues were resolved to help improve municipal services.

For Citizens

  • Create an account to start reporting issues
  • Submit detailed reports with photos and location
  • Track your issues from submission to resolution
  • Rate and provide feedback on service quality

For Municipal Staff

  • Access all reported issues in one centralized dashboard
  • Assign issues to appropriate departments
  • Update citizens on resolution progress
  • Analyze feedback to improve services
